About

I am a 3rd year DPhil student at the Visual Geometry Group, University of Oxford funded by Naver Labs Europe. My supervisors are Diane Larlus (Naver Labs Europe) and Andrea Vedaldi (Oxford University).

My research focuses on discovering and representing the semantic and geometric structure of object categories from images or videos.
