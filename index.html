About

I work as a Research Scientist at Meta AI Research London. Previously I was a DPhil student at the Visual Geometry Group, University of Oxford funded by Naver Labs Europe. My supervisors were Diane Larlus (Naver Labs Europe) and Andrea Vedaldi (University of Oxford).

My research is focused around deep learning for 3D reconstruction (creating virtual twins) and 3D generative AI.
