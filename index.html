About

I work as a Research Scientist at Facebook AI Research London. Previously I was a DPhil student at the Visual Geometry Group, University of Oxford funded by Naver Labs Europe. My supervisors were Diane Larlus (Naver Labs Europe) and Andrea Vedaldi (University of Oxford).

My research focuses on discovering and representing the semantic and geometric structure of object categories from images or videos.
